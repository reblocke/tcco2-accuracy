# Clinical Scope Review

- Identify intended use, audience, and non-goals before changing copy or interpretation.
- Keep language educational and decision-support oriented; avoid diagnosis, treatment directives, or claims of clinical validation unless documented.
- Ensure limitations and assumptions remain visible in README, docs, UI, or footer as appropriate.
- Flag any new workflow that stores, transmits, or links patient-entered values.
- Record material scope changes in clinical-scope docs or `docs/DECISIONS.md`.
