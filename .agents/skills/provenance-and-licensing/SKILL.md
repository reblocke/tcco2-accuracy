# Provenance And Licensing

- Preserve raw external inputs; write cleaned or derived outputs separately.
- For external artifacts, record source URL or citation, retrieval date, checksum when practical, license, and notes.
- Do not copy figures, tables, or substantial text unless the license and attribution allow it.
- Keep code, data, manuscript, and generated-figure licensing clear when they differ.
- Prefer machine-readable provenance files for reusable data and concise human-readable notes in docs.
